# HUS Textile Services and Usage \(2022–Present\)

## HUS Employee Count \(2022–2025\)

HUS \(Helsinki University Hospital\) is Finland’s largest healthcare provider, employing tens of thousands of staff\. The personnel count has remained high and even grown slightly in recent years:

- **2022:** Approximately **26,528 employees** at year\-end[arviointikertomushus\.fi](https://arviointikertomushus.fi/tag/henkilosto/#:~:text=Henkil%C3%B6st%C3%B6resurssit%20ja%20niiden%20riitt%C3%A4vyys)\.
- **2023:** About **27,500 employees** by year\-end[sttinfo\.fi](https://www.sttinfo.fi/tiedote/70131752/husin-vuosi-2023-potilasmaarat-kasvoivat-ja-suositteluindeksi-nousi?publisherId=23980819&lang=fi#:~:text=HUSin%20henkil%C3%B6st%C3%B6m%C3%A4%C3%A4r%C3%A4%20kasvoi%20viime%20vuodesta,paranivat%20ja%20my%C3%B6s%20sairauspoissaolot%20v%C3%A4heniv%C3%A4t) \(an increase driven mainly by growth in nursing staff\)\.
- **2024:** Around **26,926 employees** \(according to 2024 financial data\)[asiakastieto\.fi](https://www.asiakastieto.fi/yritykset/fi/hus-yhtyma/15675350/taloustiedot#:~:text=HUS)\.

These figures make HUS the biggest hospital employer in the country\. The transition to the new wellbeing county system in 2023 did not significantly reduce HUS’s staff size – HUS continues to employ around **27,000 healthcare professionals** in 2025[sttinfo\.fi](https://www.sttinfo.fi/tiedote/70131752/husin-vuosi-2023-potilasmaarat-kasvoivat-ja-suositteluindeksi-nousi?publisherId=23980819&lang=fi#:~:text=HUSissa%20saa%20vuosittain%20hoitoa%20l%C3%A4hes,harvinaisten%20ja%20vaikeiden%20sairauksien%20hoito)\.

## Garment and Textile Volumes

Supporting such a large workforce and patient population requires a massive volume of textiles \(staff uniforms, patient garments, bed linens, surgical textiles, etc\.\)\. HUS’s centralized laundry service handles **on the order of tens of millions of textile items annually**:

- Approximately **125,000 textile items are laundered per day**[puro\.fi](https://www.puro.fi/merkityksellista-yhteiskunnalle-ja-tyontekijoille-monimuotoinen-ja-hyvinvoiva-henkilosto-on-puron-toiminnan-sydan/#:~:text=Kolme%20toimipistett%C3%A4%2C%20400%20ty%C3%B6ntekij%C3%A4%C3%A4%2C%2024,osaltaan%20suomalaisen%20terveydenhuollon%20rattaat%20py%C3%B6rim%C3%A4ss%C3%A4) on average\. This corresponds to roughly **45 million pieces per year**, reflecting the scale of HUS’s operations\. For example, one HUS laundry facility \(Kerava\) processed about **7 million kg** of hospital laundry in a year \(2016\)[slideshare\.net](https://www.slideshare.net/slideshow/juhapekka-paavola-tehokkaat-energiaratkaisut/67115047#:~:text=Image%3A%20,Esimerkki%3A%20pesulan%20j%C3%A4teveden%20l%C3%A4mm%C3%B6n%20talteenotto), and the total volume for the region reached an estimated ~9 million kg&#x2F;year by the late 2010s\.
- **Daily uniform use:** Clinical staff are provided hospital uniforms which are washed by the employer[hus\.fi](https://www.hus.fi/sites/default/files/2024-03/tyovaatetus-7_2023.pdf#:~:text=Ty%C3%B6nantaja%20antaa%20ty%C3%B6ntekij%C3%B6illeen%20ty%C3%B6,Kor%02vakoruja)[hus\.fi](https://www.hus.fi/sites/default/files/2024-03/tyovaatetus-7_2023.pdf#:~:text=Ty%C3%B6pisteasuun%20kuuluvat%20lyhythihainen%20paita%2C%20housut,ty%C3%B6pisteasussa%20voi%20kulkea%20sairaala%02alueella%2C%20eik%C3%A4)\. Work attire \(e\.g\. tunic and trousers\) is **changed daily** – or even more often if needed – to maintain hygiene[hus\.fi](https://www.hus.fi/sites/default/files/2024-03/tyovaatetus-7_2023.pdf#:~:text=Ty%C3%B6pisteasuun%20kuuluvat%20lyhythihainen%20paita%2C%20housut,ty%C3%B6pisteasussa%20voi%20kulkea%20sairaala%02alueella%2C%20eik%C3%A4)\. \(Personal clothing or long\-sleeved shirts are not allowed in patient care areas; only the hospital\-issued short\-sleeve workwear is used[hus\.fi](https://www.hus.fi/sites/default/files/2024-03/tyovaatetus-7_2023.pdf#:~:text=HUSin%20ty%C3%B6asua%20,yksik%C3%B6iss%C3%A4%20ja%20potilaiden%20luona%20kotik%C3%A4ynneill%C3%A4)[hus\.fi](https://www.hus.fi/sites/default/files/2024-03/tyovaatetus-7_2023.pdf#:~:text=Ty%C3%B6pisteasuun%20kuuluvat%20lyhythihainen%20paita%2C%20housut,ty%C3%B6pisteasussa%20voi%20kulkea%20sairaala%02alueella%2C%20eik%C3%A4)\.\) This practice ensures infection control but also means each healthcare employee generates at least one set of used garments for laundry per shift\.
- In addition to staff uniforms, HUS’s textile services clean **patient linens, surgical textiles, and other reusable fabrics**\. The COVID\-19 pandemic kept demand for textile processing high – HUS noted that textile service volumes remained strong during the pandemic due to increased usage[paatokset\.vantaa\.fi](https://paatokset.vantaa.fi/ktwebscr/fileshow?doctype=3&docid=2496133#:~:text=my%C3%B6t%C3%A4,Tuotantoa%C2%A0on%20pystytty%C2%A0jatkamaan%C2%A0kuormitustarpeen%C2%A0mukaan%C2%A0yhti%C3%B6n%C2%A0eri%C2%A0toimipisteiden%C2%A0v%C3%A4lill%C3%A4%2C%C2%A0mi%20k%C3%A4%C2%A0mahdollistaa%C2%A0kaikkien%C2%A0toimipisteiden%C2%A0tilojen%C2%A0ja%20laitteiden%C2%A0tehokkaamman%C2%A0k%C3%A4yt%C3%B6n%C2%A0ja%C2%A0toimintavarmuuden%C2%A0parantamisen%C2%A0ilman%C2%A0merkitt%C3%A4v)\.

Overall, the combination of thousands of workers changing garments daily and the constant turnover of patient bedding and other linens results in **very high textile throughput**\. The ~125k articles washed each day underscore HUS’s enormous textile needs[puro\.fi](https://www.puro.fi/merkityksellista-yhteiskunnalle-ja-tyontekijoille-monimuotoinen-ja-hyvinvoiva-henkilosto-on-puron-toiminnan-sydan/#:~:text=Kolme%20toimipistett%C3%A4%2C%20400%20ty%C3%B6ntekij%C3%A4%C3%A4%2C%2024,osaltaan%20suomalaisen%20terveydenhuollon%20rattaat%20py%C3%B6rim%C3%A4ss%C3%A4)\.

## Textile Service Arrangements \(Laundry &amp; Procurement\)

HUS manages its textile and laundry needs through a dedicated in\-house company and strategic procurement:

- **In\-house laundry company – Puro Tekstiilihuoltopalvelut:** Most hospital laundry and workwear services are provided by **Puro Tekstiilihuoltopalvelut Oy**, which is part of the HUS Group\. Puro was established in late 2020 \(operations began 1\.1\.2021\) by merging Uudenmaan Sairaalapesula Oy with another laundry unit[paatokset\.vantaa\.fi](https://paatokset.vantaa.fi/ktwebscr/fileshow?doctype=3&docid=2496133#:~:text=Puro%C2%A0Tekstiilihuoltopalvelut%C2%A0Oy%C2%A0on%C2%A0perustettu%C2%A020.10.2020.%C2%A0Yhti%C3%B6%C2%A0tuottaa%C2%A0pesulapa%20lveluita%C2%A0omistajilleen%C2%A0Uudenmaan%2C%C2%A0Kymenlaakson%C2%A0sek%C3%A4%20Varsinais,periaatteella%C2%A0omistajiinsa%C2%A0n%C3%A4hden)\. HUS is the majority shareholder \(about **50\.35% ownership** as of 2022\)[paatokset\.vantaa\.fi](https://paatokset.vantaa.fi/ktwebscr/fileshow?doctype=3&docid=2496133#:~:text=Puro%C2%A0Tekstiilihuoltopalvelut%C2%A0Oy%C2%A0on%C2%A0perustettu%C2%A020.10.2020.%C2%A0Yhti%C3%B6%C2%A0tuottaa%C2%A0pesulapa%20lveluita%C2%A0omistajilleen%C2%A0Uudenmaan%2C%C2%A0Kymenlaakson%C2%A0sek%C3%A4%20Varsinais,periaatteella%C2%A0omistajiinsa%C2%A0n%C3%A4hden), with the rest owned by several hospital districts, cities, and welfare regions – so Puro operates as an **in\-house \(“sidosyksikkö”\) service provider** to its public owners[paatokset\.vantaa\.fi](https://paatokset.vantaa.fi/ktwebscr/fileshow?doctype=3&docid=2496133#:~:text=Yhti%C3%B6n%C2%A0toimiala%20Pesulatoiminta%2C%C2%A0tekstiilien%C2%A0vuokraus%2C%C2%A0erilaisten%C2%A0tekstiilihuoltopalvelujen%C2%A0tarjo%20aminen%2C%C2%A0tekstiilien%20tilaus,Yhti%C3%B6%C2%A0voi%C2%A0omistaa%C2%A0ja%C2%A0hallita%C2%A0kiinte%C3%A4%C3%A4%C2%A0omaisuutta%C2%A0ja%C2%A0arvopapereit%20a%3B%20Henkil%C3%B6st%C3%B6%20Keskim%C3%A4%C3%A4rin%C2%A0382)[paatokset\.vantaa\.fi](https://paatokset.vantaa.fi/ktwebscr/fileshow?doctype=3&docid=2496133#:~:text=Puro%C2%A0Tekstiilihuoltopalvelut%C2%A0Oy%C2%A0on%C2%A0perustettu%C2%A020.10.2020.%C2%A0Yhti%C3%B6%C2%A0tuottaa%C2%A0pesulapa%20lveluita%C2%A0omistajilleen%C2%A0Uudenmaan%2C%C2%A0Kymenlaakson%C2%A0sek%C3%A4%20Varsinais,periaatteella%C2%A0omistajiinsa%C2%A0n%C3%A4hden)\. This structure allows HUS to obtain laundry&#x2F;textile services without open external tender, as Puro qualifies as an internal unit under procurement law\. Puro’s mission covers **laundry processing, textile rental, ordering and restocking services, as well as distribution and storage solutions** for hospital textiles[paatokset\.vantaa\.fi](https://paatokset.vantaa.fi/ktwebscr/fileshow?doctype=3&docid=2496133#:~:text=Yhti%C3%B6n%C2%A0toimiala%20Pesulatoiminta%2C%C2%A0tekstiilien%C2%A0vuokraus%2C%C2%A0erilaisten%C2%A0tekstiilihuoltopalvelujen%C2%A0tarjo%20aminen%2C%C2%A0tekstiilien%20tilaus,Yhti%C3%B6%C2%A0voi%C2%A0omistaa%C2%A0ja%C2%A0hallita%C2%A0kiinte%C3%A4%C3%A4%C2%A0omaisuutta%C2%A0ja%C2%A0arvopapereit%20a%3B%20Henkil%C3%B6st%C3%B6%20Keskim%C3%A4%C3%A4rin%C2%A0382)\. In practice, Puro supplies clean workwear, patient clothing, bed linens, surgical drapes, etc\., and collects&#x2F;cleans the used textiles in a closed\-loop service\.
- **Scale and capacity:** Puro Tekstiilihuoltopalvelut is the **leading healthcare laundry service in Finland**, serving a geographic area covering nearly **half of the country’s population**[puro\.fi](https://www.puro.fi/puro/ajankohtaista/#:~:text=Tiedote%3A%20Me%20olemme%20nyt%20Puro)\. It operates **three modern laundry facilities** – located in **Kerava, Kotka, and Turku** – to ensure reliable service across Southern Finland[puro\.fi](https://www.puro.fi/puro/meista-tarkemmin/#:~:text=T%C3%A4rke%C3%A4%20osa%20toimivaa%20terveydenhuoltoa)\. Thanks to multiple plants and large processing capacity, Puro can handle the huge volumes with high reliability \(it’s deemed a critical support service for healthcare\)\. As of 2023, Puro employs around **400 staff** across these sites[puro\.fi](https://www.puro.fi/merkityksellista-yhteiskunnalle-ja-tyontekijoille-monimuotoinen-ja-hyvinvoiva-henkilosto-on-puron-toiminnan-sydan/#:~:text=Kolme%20toimipistett%C3%A4%2C%20400%20ty%C3%B6ntekij%C3%A4%C3%A4%2C%2024,osaltaan%20suomalaisen%20terveydenhuollon%20rattaat%20py%C3%B6rim%C3%A4ss%C3%A4) and processes ~125k textiles each day for its client hospitals\. The company maintains ISO\-certified quality and hygiene standards \(e\.g\. ISO 14001 environmental management and EN 14065 laundry hygiene\) given the strict infection control requirements[mercell\.com](https://www.mercell.com/fi-fi/hankinta/142777055/aulapalvelun-tyovaatteet-hankinta.aspx#:~:text=4,tuotem%C3%A4%C3%A4rien%20muutokset%2C%20lis%C3%A4tilaukset%20ja%20palautukset)[puro\.fi](https://www.puro.fi/puro/meista-tarkemmin/#:~:text=kuormittavaksi)\.
- **Uniform procurement and other textiles:** Since Puro provides a rental textile pool, HUS typically does *not* purchase individual uniforms for each employee; instead, Puro owns and supplies the garments\. HUS nurses, doctors, and other staff simply pick up fresh uniforms on\-site and return soiled ones for washing\. However, HUS does occasionally use public tenders for special textile needs\. For example, HUS’s support services unit has tendered contracts for **outsourced uniform rental** for specific roles \(such as doormen and lobby staff\)\. In one 2020 inquiry, HUS planned to rent work outfits for ~160 front\-desk personnel as a service[mercell\.com](https://www.mercell.com/fi-fi/hankinta/142777055/aulapalvelun-tyovaatteet-hankinta.aspx#:~:text=Lyhyt%20kuvaus)[mercell\.com](https://www.mercell.com/fi-fi/hankinta/142777055/aulapalvelun-tyovaatteet-hankinta.aspx#:~:text=olevat%20ehdottomat%20vaatimukset%201,EN%2014065%20mukaisesti) – including laundry and delivery – to supplement its in\-house services\. Generally, the bulk of HUS’s textile handling \(clinical workwear and hospital linens\) remains with Puro, while niche or non\-medical apparel can be sourced via procurement if needed\.
- **Sustainability and circularity:** HUS and Puro are also pursuing sustainability in textile use\. Reusable textiles are used extensively to reduce waste \(as opposed to disposable single\-use garments\)\. Worn\-out textiles are increasingly **recycled or repurposed**: Puro actively works on recycling its discarded textiles[puro\.fi](https://www.puro.fi/puro/meista-tarkemmin/#:~:text=Noudatamme%20ymp%C3%A4rist%C3%B6ohjelmaamme%2C%20jonka%20tavoitteena%20on,Pyrimme%20aktiivisesti%20kierr%C3%A4tt%C3%A4m%C3%A4%C3%A4n%20poistotekstiileit%C3%A4mme) and has participated in pilots to give “end\-of\-life” hospital textiles a new life[puro\.fi](https://www.puro.fi/puro/ajankohtaista/#:~:text=Poistotekstiilien%20uusi%20el%C3%A4m%C3%A4)\. The HUS Environmental Program emphasizes circular economy practices, and textile services are part of that effort \(e\.g\. recycling of old linens into recovery materials\)[puro\.fi](https://www.puro.fi/puro/meista-tarkemmin/#:~:text=Noudatamme%20ymp%C3%A4rist%C3%B6ohjelmaamme%2C%20jonka%20tavoitteena%20on,Pyrimme%20aktiivisesti%20kierr%C3%A4tt%C3%A4m%C3%A4%C3%A4n%20poistotekstiileit%C3%A4mme)\. This not only cuts waste but also reduces the need for procuring new textiles\.
