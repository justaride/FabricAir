# Finnish Stakeholders for High\-Quality Used Polyester Fiber Sourcing

## Executive Summary

Finland offers exceptional opportunities for sourcing high\-quality used polyester textiles, with approximately 100 million kg of textile waste generated annually\. The country&#39;s advanced circular economy infrastructure, mandatory textile collection since 2023, and concentration of industrial textile service companies create ideal conditions for establishing a reliable polyester fiber supply chain\.

## Key Success Factors

### Regulatory Drivers

- **Mandatory textile collection** since January 2023 \(2 years ahead of EU\)
- **Extended Producer Responsibility \(EPR\)** implementation 2024\-2025
- **EPR fees:** €0\.12\-0\.24 per kg creating economic incentives
- **Carbon neutrality goal:** 2035 national target

### Infrastructure Advantages

- **Telaketju Network:** 26\+ companies in textile recycling ecosystem
- **Government funding:** €60\-120 million annual mechanical recycling market
- **Business Finland** and **Sitra** circular economy programs
- **Public procurement:** €35 billion annually with sustainability criteria
