# Finnish Textile Regulations \- Technical List

## Current Regulations \(In Force\)

### National Legislation

1. **Waste Act 646&#x2F;2011** \- Framework law establishing waste hierarchy and producer responsibility principles \([Finlex](https://www.finlex.fi/fi/lainsaadanto/saadoskokoelma/2021/978)\)
2. **Government Decree 978&#x2F;2021** \- Mandates separate textile collection from January 1, 2023 for households and July 1, 2022 for businesses \([Zero Waste Europe](https://zerowasteeurope.eu/wp-content/uploads/2022/11/ZWE-EWWR2022-factsheet_Finland-textiles.pdf)\)
3. **Landfill Ban \(2016\)** \- Prohibits landfilling of textile waste, forcing all materials into recovery pathways \([EastCham Finland](https://www.eastcham.fi/finnishwastemanagement/municipal-solid-waste/recycling-and-recovery/textile/)\)

### EU Regulations Applicable in Finland

1. **REACH Regulation \(EC\) No 1907&#x2F;2006** \- Chemical safety requirements for all textiles including recycled materials \([Europa](https://echa.europa.eu/regulations/reach/legislation)\)
2. **REACH Annex XVII, Entry 72** \- Restricts 33 CMR chemicals in textiles with human skin contact \([Tukes](https://tukes.fi/en/chemicals/reach)\)
3. **Waste Framework Directive 2008&#x2F;98&#x2F;EC \(amended 2018&#x2F;851\)** \- Requires separate textile collection by January 2025 \(Finland compliant since 2023\) \([EUR\-Lex](https://eur-lex.europa.eu/legal-content/EN/TXT/?uri=CELEX:32018L0851)\)
4. **Waste Shipment Regulation \(EC\) 1013&#x2F;2006** \- Controls textile waste exports requiring permits for international shipments \([Finnish Environment Institute](https://www.ymparisto.fi/en)\)

### Standards and Safety Requirements

1. **SFS\-EN 14682** \- Safety requirements for children&#39;s clothing cords and drawstrings \([Tukes](https://tukes.fi/en)\)
2. **SFS\-EN 71\-3** \- Safety requirements for toy components including textile materials \([Tukes](https://tukes.fi/en)\)

## Upcoming Regulations \(2025\-2030\)

### EU Legislation in Development

1. **Extended Producer Responsibility for Textiles \(2025\-2027\)** \- Provisional agreement reached February 2025, implementation expected 2027\-2028 \([Council of EU](https://www.consilium.europa.eu/en/press/press-releases/2025/02/19/council-and-parliament-agree-to-reduce-food-waste-and-set-new-rules-on-waste-textile/)\)
2. **Digital Product Passport \(2026\-2027\)** \- Mandatory QR codes with material composition and recycling information \([STJM](https://www.stjm.fi/en/uutiset/finnish-textile-fashions-views-on-the-extended-producer-responsibility-scheme-for-textiles/)\)
3. **Ecodesign for Sustainable Products Regulation \(2027\-2028\)** \- Minimum durability, recyclability, and recycled content requirements \([SGS](https://www.sgs.com/en-fi/news/2025/06/safeguards-07325-eu-releases-espr-working-plan-2025-2030-prioritizing-textiles-for-sustainability)\)
4. **Destruction Ban for Unsold Textiles \(2026&#x2F;2030\)** \- Prohibits destroying unsold inventory \(2026 large companies, 2030 SMEs\) \([European Commission](https://environment.ec.europa.eu/strategy/textiles-strategy_en)\)
5. **EU Strategy for Sustainable and Circular Textiles \(2030 targets\)** \- Overarching strategy requiring textiles be durable, recyclable, and largely made from recycled fibers \([European Commission](https://ec.europa.eu/commission/presscorner/detail/en/qanda_22_2015)\)

## Finnish Policy Initiatives

1. **Circular Economy Act \(Under Development 2024\-2025\)** \- Will replace current Waste Act with comprehensive lifecycle legislation \([Ministry of Environment](https://ym.fi/en/waste)\)
2. **Green Deal for Circular Economy** \- Voluntary agreement engaging 90 stakeholders in textile circularity \([Ministry of Environment](https://ym.fi/en/waste)\)
3. **Basel Convention Amendment \(Proposed\)** \- Finland advocates to subject textile waste to Basel Convention prior informed consent requirements \([Government\.se](https://www.government.se)\)
