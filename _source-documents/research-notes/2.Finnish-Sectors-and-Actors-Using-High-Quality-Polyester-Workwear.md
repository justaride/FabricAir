# Finnish Sectors and Actors Using High\-Quality Polyester Workwear

## Healthcare Sector \(Primary white polyester users\)

### Major Hospital Districts

- **HUS \(Hospital District of Helsinki and Uusimaa\)** \- Finland&#39;s largest healthcare provider with 21 hospitals, 22,000\+ employees, and 230,000 garment pieces procured
- **TYKS \(Turku University Hospital\)** \- Major university hospital serving Southwest Finland
- **TAYS \(Tampere University Hospital\)** \- Central Finland&#39;s primary academic medical center
- **OYS \(Oulu University Hospital\)** \- Northern Finland&#39;s largest healthcare facility
- **KYS \(Kuopio University Hospital\)** \- Eastern Finland&#39;s main university hospital

### Private Healthcare Chains

- **Terveystalo Oyj** \- Finland&#39;s largest private healthcare provider with 60,000 garment pieces using 90% recycled polyester
- **Mehiläinen Oy** \- Leading private healthcare operator with over 90% brand recognition nationally
- **Pihlajalinna Oyj** \- Major private healthcare and social services provider

## Food Processing Industry \(High white polyester concentration\)

- **Valio Oy** \- National dairy giant requiring HACCP\-compliant white uniforms across production facilities
- **Fazer Group** \- Major food manufacturer with strict hygiene standards requiring white workwear
- **Atria Plc** \- Employing 2,600 people with documented RFID\-tracked workwear through Lindström
- **HKScan Finland** \- Leading meat and ready\-meal producer with high hygiene standards
- **Saarioinen** \- Food processor managing 3,000 garments \(2\.5 tonnes\) through textile services
- **Paulig Group** \- Coffee and food company with multiple production facilities
- **Raisio Oyj** \- Food and nutrition company requiring food\-safe uniforms

## Pharmaceutical &amp; Cleanroom \(Near 100% white polyester\)

- **Orion Corporation** \- Finnish pharmaceutical manufacturer with facilities in Espoo, Turku, and Hanko requiring cleanroom garments
- **Bayer Finland** \- Pharmaceutical operations with cleanroom requirements
- **Santen \(Tampere\)** \- Ophthalmological pharmaceutical production requiring ISO\-standard cleanroom wear

## Electronics &amp; Semiconductor Manufacturing \(White cleanroom textiles\)

- **Okmetic \(Vantaa\)** \- Silicon wafer manufacturer with significant cleanroom operations
- **Murata Electronics Finland \(Vantaa\)** \- Operating ISO 4\-8 cleanrooms requiring filament polyester garments

## Laboratory &amp; Diagnostics \(White lab coats\)

- **Fimlab Laboratoriot** \- Finland&#39;s largest laboratory chain with over 100 locations
- **SYNLAB Finland** \- International laboratory chain with significant Finnish operations
