## The Opportunity

FabricAir can access **56,000-62,000 tonnes/year** of recycled polyester capacity across three Nordic countries by 2028-2029.

| Country | Partner | Capacity | Timeline |
|---------|---------|----------|----------|
| Finland | Rester | 6-12k t/yr | Now |
| Norway | NTG | 30k t/yr | ~2029 |
| Sweden | Rewin | 20k t/yr | Oct 2027 |

**Start with Rester (Finland)** — they're operational today.

| Finding | Impact |
|---------|--------|
| **Lindström uses 65:35 blends** | 400 t/year volume INCOMPATIBLE with FabricAir |

## Material Requirements

FabricAir requires:

- **Material:** 100% Polyester (PET)
- **Weight:** ~300 g/m²
- **Color:** White preferred
- **Source:** Durable workwear, uniforms
- **NOT suitable:** Polyester/cotton blends
