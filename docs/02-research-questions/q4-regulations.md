## Regulatory Summary

Finland is **2 years ahead of EU requirements**, having implemented mandatory textile collection in January 2023. The regulatory landscape is shifting from municipal responsibility to **Extended Producer Responsibility (EPR)** expected 2027-2028.

---

## Current Regulations (2023-2025)

### National Legislation

| Regulation | Key Requirement | Effective Date |
|------------|-----------------|----------------|
| **Waste Act 646/2011** | Framework law, waste hierarchy | In force |
| **Government Decree 978/2021** | Mandatory separate textile collection | Jan 1, 2023 (households) |
| | | Jul 1, 2022 (businesses) |
| **Landfill Ban (2016)** | No textile landfilling | In force |

### Collection System Requirements

**Municipal (Household) System:**
- Municipalities must provide sufficient regional collection points
- Materials prioritized: reuse â†’ mechanical recycling
- 97% population coverage through 31 waste management areas
- LSJH coordinates national processing

**Business System:**
- Companies must arrange separate collection "as far as possible"
- Companies with >â‚¬2,000 annual waste service needs must use **Materiaalitori.fi** platform
- Emergency provision: Can request LSJH services if no commercial options within 2 weeks

### Chemical Safety Requirements

**REACH Regulation:**
| Requirement | Detail |
|-------------|--------|
| SVHC content limit | <0.1% by weight |
| Entry 72, Annex XVII | 33 CMR chemicals restricted in skin-contact textiles |
| Candidate list | 250 substances (as of June 2025) |

**Finnish Standards:**
- SFS-EN 14682: Children's clothing safety
- SFS-EN 71-3: Toy safety (textile components)
- Formaldehyde, azo dyes, nickel, phthalates specifically restricted

## Upcoming Regulations (2025-2030)

### Extended Producer Responsibility (EPR)

**Status:** Provisional EU agreement reached **February 19, 2025**

**Timeline:**
| Phase | Date | Action |
|-------|------|--------|
| EU directive adoption | 2025 | Formal adoption |
| Transposition period | 2026-2027 | 20 months for national laws |
| Full EPR operational | **2027-2028** | PROs established |
| Micro-enterprise compliance | +12 months | Additional period |

**Key Changes:**

| Aspect | Current (2023-2025) | Future EPR (2027+) |
|--------|---------------------|-------------------|
| Funding | Municipal taxes | Producer fees |
| Collection management | Municipalities | PROs |
| Cost coverage | Taxpayers | Manufacturers, importers, distributors |

**Eco-Modulation Mechanism:**
Producer fees will vary based on:
- âœ“ Durability and product lifetime
- âœ“ Recyclability (mono-materials favored)

### Ecodesign for Sustainable Products Regulation (2027-2028)

| Requirement | Timeline |
|-------------|----------|
| Mandatory durability standards | 2027-2028 |
| Minimum recyclability standards | 2027-2028 |
| Minimum recycled fiber content | TBD |
| Ban on destroying unsold textiles | 2026 (large), 2030 (SMEs) |
| Microplastic prevention (synthetics) | TBD |

### Export Controls (Proposed)

Finland advocates (with Nordic partners) to subject textile waste to **Basel Convention** controls:
- Would require prior informed consent for exports
- Currently 82% of Finnish textile waste processed domestically
