# Q5: Collection and Reuse Initiatives

## Question

> If there are public and/or private initiatives in Finland to collect and reuse polyester textiles in Finland today, what are their status, their ambition and main contact points?

## Status: Research Available

## Overview

Finland operates **Europe's most advanced textile recycling ecosystem** with:
- Mandatory collection since January 2023 (2 years ahead of EU)
- 70-100 million kg textile waste processed annually
- Integrated public-private partnerships
- Specific capabilities for polyester recycling at industrial scale
