## 1. Energy Recovery (Incineration) - 60-61%

**Volume:** ~40,000 tonnes household textiles to mixed waste annually

### What gets incinerated:
| Stream | Volume | Notes |
|--------|--------|-------|
| Mixed household waste | Majority | 96%+ of textiles in mixed waste |
| Defence Forces uniforms | 43,000 kg (80% of 53,408 kg) | FR treatments limit recycling |
| Contaminated industrial | Variable | Oil-stained, chemically treated |
| Post-sorting waste | 40% of sorted | Not suitable for recycling |

### Industrial laundry disposal breakdown:
- **71% incinerated**
- 26.7% material recovery
- 1.8% reused
- 0.5% other

- Source: Household collection
