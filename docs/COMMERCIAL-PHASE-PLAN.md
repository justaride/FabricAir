# Commercial Phase Plan: Nordic Circular Polyester Supply Chain

**Document Date:** 2025-11-29
**Phase:** Research Complete → Commercial Development
**Timeline:** Q1 2025 - Q4 2025
**Owner:** FabricAir ESG / Commercial Team

---

## Executive Summary

The Commercial Phase transforms research findings into operational supply agreements. The goal is to establish reliable sourcing of recycled polyester fiber from Nordic partners by end of 2025.

**Target Outcome:**
- 2-3 signed feedstock agreements
- Pilot production with recycled Nordic polyester
- Validated supply chain ready for scaling

### 1.2 Discovery Meetings

**Objective:** Understand capabilities, capacity, and interest

**Meeting Agenda (60 min):**
1. FabricAir presentation (15 min)
   - Company overview
   - Material requirements (100% PET, ~300 g/m², white)
   - Volume projections
   - Sustainability commitments

2. Partner presentation (20 min)
   - Current operations
   - Technology capabilities
   - Available capacity
   - Existing customers/commitments

3. Alignment discussion (20 min)
   - Technical compatibility
   - Timeline alignment
   - Potential collaboration models
   - Next steps

4. Action items (5 min)

**Key Questions to Ask:**
- What is your current output specification (fiber length, purity, color)?
- What capacity is available for new customers?
- What feedstock sources produce 100% white polyester?
- What is your typical lead time and minimum order?
- Are you GRS certified or planning certification?

## Phase 2: Technical Validation (Weeks 4-10)

### 2.1 Sample Requests

**Objective:** Obtain material samples for testing

| Partner | Sample Type | Quantity | Specification Request |
|---------|-------------|----------|----------------------|
| Rester | Recycled PET fiber | 5-10 kg | White, 100% PET, fiber specs |
| NTG | Defibrated fiber | 5-10 kg | Mixed and pure PET samples |

**Sample Request Specifications:**
- Fiber composition (% polyester)
- Fiber length distribution
- Color (white/off-white preferred)
- Contaminant levels
- Moisture content
- Any treatments applied

### 2.2 Laboratory Testing

**Objective:** Validate material compatibility with FabricAir production

**Test Protocol:**

| Test | Purpose | Pass Criteria |
|------|---------|---------------|
| Fiber composition | Verify 100% PET | ≥98% polyester |
| Fiber length | Spinning suitability | Within spec range |
| Tensile strength | Durability | Meet product spec |
| Color consistency | Visual quality | White/acceptable shade |
| Contaminant analysis | Purity check | Below threshold |
| Melt flow index | Processing compatibility | Within range |

**Testing Partners:**
- FabricAir internal lab (initial screening)
- External textile testing lab (certification)
- Production trial (small batch)

### 2.3 Production Trial

**Objective:** Test recycled fiber in actual production

**Trial Parameters:**
- Batch size: 100-500 kg recycled fiber
- Blend ratio: Start 10-30% recycled, increase if successful
- Product: Non-critical SKU for initial trial
- Quality monitoring: Full QC at each stage

**Success Criteria:**
- No production issues (web breaks, defects)
- Product meets performance specifications
- Visual quality acceptable
- Cost within acceptable range

## Phase 3: Commercial Negotiation (Weeks 8-16)

### 3.1 Pricing Framework

**Objective:** Establish commercial terms

**Pricing Components:**

| Component | Typical Range | Notes |
|-----------|---------------|-------|
| Base rPET fiber price | €1.50-2.00/kg | 50-70% premium over virgin |
| Volume discount | 5-15% | For annual commitments |
| Quality premium/discount | ±10% | Based on purity, color |
| Logistics | €0.10-0.30/kg | Distance dependent |
| Certification (GRS) | Included or +5% | Required for marketing |

**Negotiation Levers:**
- Volume commitment (annual tonnage)
- Contract duration (1-3 years)
- Exclusivity arrangements
- Joint development investment
- Marketing co-branding

### 3.2 Contract Structure

**Key Terms to Negotiate:**

| Term | FabricAir Interest | Typical Range |
|------|-------------------|---------------|
| **Volume commitment** | Flexibility | 50-200 tonnes/year |
| **Price mechanism** | Stability | Fixed or indexed |
| **Quality specification** | Strict | Defined in annex |
| **Delivery schedule** | Regular | Monthly/quarterly |
| **Lead time** | Short | 4-8 weeks |
| **Minimum order** | Low | 1-5 tonnes |
| **Payment terms** | Standard | Net 30-60 |
| **Force majeure** | Balanced | Standard clause |
| **Termination** | Reasonable | 90-180 days notice |

### 3.3 Risk Allocation

| Risk | Mitigation |
|------|------------|
| Supply disruption | Multi-supplier strategy (2-3 partners) |
| Quality variability | Defined specs + incoming inspection |
| Price volatility | Fixed pricing or caps |
| Capacity constraints | Long-term agreements + priority status |
| Regulatory changes | Flexibility clauses |

## Phase 4: Pilot Projects (Weeks 12-24)

### 4.1 Pilot Scope

**Objective:** Prove concept before full-scale commitment

**Pilot Structure:**

| Element | Specification |
|---------|---------------|
| Duration | 3-6 months |
| Volume | 10-50 tonnes |
| Products | 2-3 SKUs |
| Markets | Test markets first |
| Investment | Shared development costs |

### 4.2 Pilot Phases

**Phase A: Material Integration (Month 1-2)**
- Receive first commercial shipment
- Integrate into production line
- Document process adjustments
- Quality monitoring

**Phase B: Product Validation (Month 2-4)**
- Produce pilot batch products
- Internal quality testing
- Customer samples (select accounts)
- Feedback collection

**Phase C: Market Test (Month 4-6)**
- Limited market release
- Customer feedback
- Performance monitoring
- Cost analysis

### 4.3 Pilot Success Criteria

| Criteria | Target | Measurement |
|----------|--------|-------------|
| Production yield | ≥95% | Actual vs expected output |
| Quality compliance | 100% | QC pass rate |
| Customer acceptance | Positive | Feedback scores |
| Cost variance | ≤10% | Actual vs budget |
| Supply reliability | ≥95% | On-time delivery |

## Phase 5: Supply Agreement (Weeks 20-30)

### 5.1 Agreement Types

**Option A: Direct Supply Agreement**
```
Recycler (Rester/NTG) ────→ FabricAir
- Direct relationship
- Volume commitments
- Quality specifications
- Pricing agreement
```

**Option B: Feedstock Partnership**
```
Textile Waste Source ────→ Recycler ────→ FabricAir
(Puro, Nor Tekstil)      (Processing)    (End user)
- Triangular agreement
- Feedstock specification
- Processing terms
- End product delivery
```

**Option C: Joint Development**
```
FabricAir + Recycler
- Shared investment
- Dedicated capacity
- Exclusive output
- Long-term partnership
```

### 5.2 Multi-Supplier Strategy

**Recommended Portfolio:**

| Supplier | Role | Volume Share | Timeline |
|----------|------|--------------|----------|
| Rester (Finland) | Primary | 50-60% | 2025 |
| NTG (Norway) | Secondary | 30-40% | 2025-2026 |
| Rewin (Sweden) | Future | 20-30% | 2027+ |

**Benefits:**
- Supply security (no single point of failure)
- Competitive pricing (negotiation leverage)
- Technology diversity (mechanical + chemical)
- Geographic coverage (Nordic-wide)

### 5.3 Agreement Milestones

| Milestone | Target Date | Deliverable |
|-----------|-------------|-------------|
| LOI signed | Q1 2025 | Letter of Intent with 1-2 partners |
| Trial agreement | Q2 2025 | Pilot project contract |
| Pilot complete | Q3 2025 | Validation report |
| Supply agreement | Q4 2025 | Multi-year contract |
| First commercial order | Q4 2025 | Production-scale delivery |

## Phase 6: Operations & Scaling (2026+)

### 6.1 Supply Chain Operations

**Operational Model:**

```
NORDIC FEEDSTOCK          PROCESSING              FABRICAIR
─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Finland:                  Rester (Paimio)
- Puro (healthcare)   ──→ - Sorting            ──→ FabricAir
- Touchpoint (workwear)   - Mechanical recycling    (Denmark)
- Orion (cleanroom)       - Fiber production

Norway:                   NTG (Sandefjord)
- Nor Tekstil         ──→ - Defibration        ──→ FabricAir
- Elis Norge              - Fiber production        (Denmark)
- Fretex

Sweden (2027+):           Rewin (Vänersborg)
- Elis Sweden         ──→ - Chemical recycling ──→ FabricAir
- Textilia                - Fiber production        (Denmark)
```

### 6.2 Quality Assurance

**QA Framework:**

| Stage | Activity | Frequency |
|-------|----------|-----------|
| Incoming | Fiber inspection | Every shipment |
| Incoming | Lab testing | Monthly composite |
| Production | Process monitoring | Continuous |
| Production | In-line quality | Per batch |
| Finished | Product testing | Per lot |
| Audit | Supplier audit | Annual |

### 6.3 Scaling Roadmap

| Year | Volume Target | Recycled Content | Suppliers |
|------|---------------|------------------|-----------|
| 2025 | 20-50 tonnes | 10-20% of production | 1-2 |
| 2026 | 100-200 tonnes | 30-50% of production | 2-3 |
| 2027 | 300-500 tonnes | 50-70% of production | 3+ |
| 2028+ | 500+ tonnes | 70-100% of production | 3+ |
