## The Opportunity

NTG (Norsk Tekstilgjenvinning) is the fastest-growing Nordic recycler, scaling from 2,000 to 30,000 t/year by ~2029.

| Attribute | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| Current capacity | 2,000 t/year |
| Target capacity | 30,000 t/year |
| Timeline | ~2029 (5 years from 2024) |
| Technology | Patented mechanical defibration |
| Fiber types | Polyester, cotton, wool, poly-cotton blends |

**Key innovation:** Defibration cuts fibers (not shreds) — preserves length for respinning.
