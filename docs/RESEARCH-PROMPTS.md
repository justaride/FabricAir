## Part 1: Finnish Textile Market Statistics Verification

### 1.1 Total Textile Waste Volume

**Claim to verify:** "Finland generates 86,000 tonnes of textile waste annually"

**Research Prompt:**
```
Search for official Finnish textile waste statistics from government sources:

1. Find Statistics Finland (Tilastokeskus) data on textile waste generation
   - Search: "Statistics Finland textile waste tonnes annual" OR "Tilastokeskus tekstiilijäte"
   - Look for: Annual waste statistics by category, municipal waste composition studies

2. Find Finnish Environment Institute (SYKE) textile reports
   - Search: "SYKE Finland textile waste report" OR "Suomen ympäristökeskus tekstiilit"
   - Look for: Environmental reports, waste flow studies, circular economy reports

3. Find Ministry of Environment (Ympäristöministeriö) publications
   - Search: "Finland Ministry Environment textile waste policy"
   - Look for: National waste plan, textile strategy documents

4. Cross-reference with EU Eurostat data
   - Search: "Eurostat textile waste Finland"
   - Look for: Waste statistics by country, textile category data

Deliverable: Exact figure with source URL, publication date, and methodology notes.
```

## Part 7: Primary Research Interview Guides

### 7.1 Recycler Interview (Rester/LSJH)

**Use this guide for calls with Rester or LSJH:**

```
Interview Guide: Textile Recycler

Introduction (2 min):
- FabricAir background: Danish textile ventilation manufacturer
- NCC project context: Nordic circular construction initiative
- Goal: Assess partnership potential for recycled polyester fiber supply

Volume & Capacity (10 min):
1. What is your current annual processing capacity?
2. What percentage is 100% polyester vs blends?
3. How much is available for external customers vs. existing contracts?
4. What are your expansion plans for the next 2-3 years?

Quality & Specifications (10 min):
5. Can you sort by color? What's your white/light polyester volume?
6. What fiber specifications can you provide? (denier, staple length)
7. How do you handle flame retardant and PU-coated textiles?
8. Can you provide material certifications? (GRS, Oeko-Tex)

Commercial (10 min):
9. What is your indicative pricing per kg for sorted polyester fiber?
10. What are minimum order quantities?
11. What delivery terms do you offer?
12. Who are your current customers for recycled fiber output?

Partnership (5 min):
13. Would you be open to a pilot project (e.g., 5-10 tonnes)?
14. Can we receive samples for testing?
15. Who would be our primary contact for ongoing discussions?

Deliverable: Completed interview notes with quantified answers.
```
