## The Opportunity

Sweden will have domestic polyester recycling by 2027, creating a simpler supply chain than cross-border flows.

| Timeline | Strategy |
|----------|----------|
| 2024-2027 | Cross-border to Finnish Rester |
| 2027+ | Domestic Swedish recycling via Rewin |

## Best Sources for 100% White Polyester

|---------|-----------|-------|

AstraZeneca Södertälje produces 40% of AstraZeneca's global volume — excellent source for white polyester cleanroom garments.

### Challenge: Blends

Most Swedish workwear is 65/35 poly-cotton — NOT suitable for FabricAir:

| Sector | Composition | Suitable? |
|--------|-------------|-----------|
| Cleanroom | 100% polyester | Yes |
| Healthcare | 65/35 blend | No |
| Food | 65/35 blend | No |
| Industrial | Mixed | No |
